💡 Quick Guide — Choose the right option
Non-Rooted For phones without root/Magisk. Install MicroG first, then the APK. Recommended for most users
Rooted (Magisk) For phones with root/Magisk. Flash the module in Magisk Manager. No MicroG needed.
ARM64 (64-bit) For most modern phones (2014+). Check Settings → About Phone to confirm. Recommended
ARM (32-bit) For older phones or devices with 32-bit OS. Only select if you know your CPU is arm-v7a.
